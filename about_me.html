Co-Chairs

Joshua Jeffers

President & CEO

Prior to forming J. Jeffers & Co., Josh worked in the private equity real estate industry, starting his career in the Acquisitions department of Walton Street Capital in Chicago. Joshua’s transactional experience includes nearly $1.4 billion of commercial real estate investment in U.S. and international markets, and spans office, retail, multifamily, industrial, hospitality, and mixed-use asset types.

Josh earned a Master's in Public Policy from the John F. Kennedy School of Government at Harvard University, and a BBA in Finance and Real Estate from the University of Wisconsin-Madison.

Danielle Bergner

COO & Generel Counsel

Danielle has over 15 years of experience in commercial real estate and finance and has closed over $2.0 billion in transactions nationally. Before joining J. Jeffers, Danielle was chair of the real estate practice group and office managing partner for an Am Law 200 law firm, and served as Deputy City Attorney for the City of Milwaukee. Danielle is widely recognized for her expertise with public-private partnerships and complex mixed-finance transactions involving tax credits, TIF and bond financing.

Danielle earned her law degree from Marquette University, with honors, and was an editor of the Marquette Law Review.

Melissa Goins

Vice President, Development

Melissa joined J. Jeffers in 2018 as Vice President of Development. In this role she leads some of the company's largest, most complex transactions. Melissa has extensive experience leading high-value projects, including expertise in affordable housing finance and development. She has a diverse background in real estate valuation, development, planning, finance, project management, and diversity initiatives. Her portfolio includes over $75 million of successful new development.

Melissa earned a MBA from Marquette University and is a graduate of Marquette's Associates in Commercial Real Estate (ACRE) program.

Principals

Jonathan Ward

Vice President, Development

Jonathan handles new project development, financing, due diligence and acquisitions. With experience in both the public and private sector, Jonathan has created public-private partnerships to finance some of the most complex, multi-source urban redevelopment projects in Milwaukee and Pittsburgh. He has closed dozens of New Market Tax Credit projects while at PNC Bank and for private developers.

Jonathan earned a BA in Public Administration from Grand Valley State University and a Master of Science in Public Policy and Management from Carnegie Mellon University.

Andrew C. Wilson

Vice President, Design & Construction

Andy leads the Development and Construction projects for J. Jeffers & Co., including team selection, quality control, and internal project management from pre-development through stabilization. Andy brings 15 years of design, construction management, and construction estimating experience from New York City to his new hometown of Milwaukee. He is a licensed architect (University of Notre Dame, 2003, summa cum laude), a LEED Accredited Professional, and is excited to bring all his skills to bear on the complex projects tackled by the firm.

Andrew earned a Bachelor of Architecture from University of Notre Dame.
